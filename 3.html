Tension and Ambience Soundscape


I created this soundscape in Ableton Live by mixing intense sounds from its stock packs with ambient pads. It takes you on an adventure, like exploring a mysterious cave or evading danger. With high-frequency notes and intense vibes, it builds tension and excitement, keeping you on edge.

Gothic Mansion Ambience


I composed a gothic ambience soundscape for a team creating a VR experience in an ancient gothic mansion. My task was to carefully select sounds that would create the spooky atmosphere of the mansion, drawing inspiration from classic gothic literature and buildings. By mixing eerie sounds like ominous tones, howling wind, ghostly moans and distant whispers, I made the haunted mansion feel real, giving users an immersive and chilling experience.

Pepsi Commercial Sound Re-design
