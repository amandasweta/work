Thief Animation Sound Re-Design


This project involved re-designing the audio for an animated video titled "Thief" from YouTube. The aim was to enhance the visual storytelling by adding immersive sound effects (SFX) and foley, without including any music. This re-design showcases my ability to enhance the tension and atmosphere of the animation through strategic use of sound, emphasizing key moments without overshadowing the visuals.


Role: Sound Designer

Audio Implementation for FPP Shooter Game


This project was part of my final year coursework, undertaken in collaboration with three other sound designers and a game student. Our goal was to create an immersive audio experience for a first-person shooter (FPS) game using industry-standard tools.


Role: Sound Designer


Responsibilities:
1. Sound Effects (SFX) Creation using Ableton Live
2. Foley Recording and Editing using Pro Tools
3. Implementation: using FMOD and Unity

Ring Movie Clip Sound Re-Design


This project involved re-designing the audio for a short clip from the movie "The Ring." The objective was to enhance the suspense and atmosphere of the scene by adding immersive sound effects (SFX), foley, and music. Utilizing samples of existing music tracks as a foundation, the re-design aimed to further enhance the emotional impact and tension of the scene.


Role: Sound Designer

Tension and Ambience Soundscape


I created this soundscape in Ableton Live by mixing intense sounds from its stock packs with ambient pads. It takes you on an adventure, like exploring a mysterious cave or evading danger. With high-frequency notes and intense vibes, it builds tension and excitement, keeping you on edge.

Gothic Mansion Ambience


I composed a gothic ambience soundscape for a team creating a VR experience in an ancient gothic mansion. My task was to carefully create and select sounds that would create the spooky atmosphere of the mansion, drawing inspiration from classic gothic literature and buildings. By mixing eerie sounds like ominous tones, howling wind, ghostly moans and distant whispers, I made the haunted mansion feel real, giving users an immersive and chilling experience.
